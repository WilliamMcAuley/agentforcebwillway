What is this site?

  • This is a website hosted(think godaddy.com) by a Salesforce product called Heroku.
  • Behind the sences you can easily modify anything and everything on the site, although it is a step up from basic word press.
  • The cool thing is this website is directly connected to my salesforce org where I track job applications
  • I can even update job applications dirrectly from this site, however since its public all the fields are read only
